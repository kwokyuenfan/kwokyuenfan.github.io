Developer's ESG Reputation Concern and Green Building Development

with Jianfu Shen, Eddie C.M. Hui and Yan Yee Hung

*Target: Journal of Real Estate Research*

This study investigates whether developers' concerns about their ESG reputation influence their decisions to develop green buildings and the underlying mechanisms of this relationship.
