Can Fintech Enhance Household Climate Resilience? Evidence from P2P Lending Platforms

with Jianfu Shen and Siyu Liu

*Expected to be submitted to Journal of Financial Intermediation*

This working paper examines whether access to peer-to-peer lending platforms improves households' ability to cope with climate-related shocks and build long-term climate resilience.
