Blended Finance in Global Infrastructure Projects

with Xin Chang, Nuoya Fang, Kangkang Fu and Jianfu Shen

*Expected to be submitted to The Journal of Finance*

This study examines how blended finance mechanisms—strategic use of development finance to mobilize private capital—can bridge the infrastructure funding gap and promote sustainable development outcomes globally.
