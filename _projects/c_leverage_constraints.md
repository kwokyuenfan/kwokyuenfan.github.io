Leverage Constraints and Financial Manipulation in the Real Estate Sector

with Jianfu Shen and Qing Yu

*Expected to be submitted to Journal of Real Estate Finance and Economics*

This study investigates how leverage constraints in the real estate sector create incentives for financial manipulation and examines the consequences for firm performance and market stability.
