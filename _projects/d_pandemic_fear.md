The Price of Pandemic Fear in the Residential Rental Market: Evidence from Hong Kong

with Jianfu Shen and Eddie C.M. Hui

*Expected to be submitted to Journal of Real Estate Research*

This study examines how pandemic-induced fear affected rental prices in Hong Kong's residential market, quantifying the impact of disease risk perception on housing market dynamics.
