Climate Risk and Green Transformation in Real Estate Sector

with Ting Jiang, Jianfu Shen and Eddie C.M. Hui

*Expected to be submitted to Journal of Real Estate Finance and Economics*

This study investigates how climate risk exposure drives green transformation strategies in the real estate sector and evaluates the financial implications of these strategic responses.
