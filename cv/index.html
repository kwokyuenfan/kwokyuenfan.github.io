Professional Summary

Assistant Professor of Economics and Finance at The Hang Seng University of Hong Kong (HSUHK). My research uses Corporate Finance as a lens to examine how firms balance economic gains with social welfare, with a focus on sustainability, real estate, and corporate finance. My work spans ESG engagement in REITs, the political economy of pollution, and climate resilience in property markets.
